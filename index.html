Frequently asked questions

Can I import my Anki decks?

Yes. sumi reads .apkg files directly. Open the file on your iPhone or use the in-app import. Your cards, formatting, and media are preserved.

How does spaced repetition work?

After each review, you rate how well you remembered the card. The SRS algorithm then schedules the next review — cards you know well appear days or weeks later, while difficult cards come back sooner. This is scientifically proven to improve long-term retention.

Is sumi free?

Yes. The free version includes 1 deck, 100 cards, and 2 imports. sumi pro unlocks unlimited decks, cards, imports, cram mode, reverse study, and detailed insights.

Does sumi work offline?

Completely. All data is stored locally on your device. No internet connection, no account, and no cloud sync required.

What file formats can I import?

sumi supports .apkg (Anki packages), CSV, TSV, plain text, and JSON files.

Is my data private?

Yes. sumi collects no analytics, no personal data, and uses no third-party tracking. Everything stays on your device. Read our privacy policy.
