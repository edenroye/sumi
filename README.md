# sumi

**Quiet flashcards for iPhone & iPad**

A clean, offline-first flashcard app with spaced repetition and Anki import. No accounts, no tracking — just studying.

[![Download on the App Store](https://developer.apple.com/assets/elements/badges/download-on-the-app-store.svg)](https://apps.apple.com/app/sumi-quiet-flashcards/id6759393853)

## Features

- **Spaced repetition** — built-in SRS scheduling so you review at the right time
- **Anki import** — open .apkg files directly on your iPhone or iPad
- **Cloze deletions** — create fill-in-the-blank cards with `{{curly braces}}`
- **CSV / TSV / JSON import** — bring your own data from any source
- **Offline-first** — everything stays on your device, no account required
